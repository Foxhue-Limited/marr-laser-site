# Managing Your Website — MARR Laser & Skin Clinic

Your website content is managed through **Sanity Studio**, a simple online editor. No technical knowledge is needed — if you can use a word processor, you can use Sanity.

---

## Logging In

Go to: **marr-laser.sanity.studio**

Sign in with your email address. Once in, you'll see a sidebar on the left with all your content sections.

---

## The Sidebar

| Section | What it controls |
|---|---|
| **Site Settings** | Clinic name, tagline, opening hours, social media links, contact details |
| **Pages** | About page carousel images |
| **Services** | All your treatments — descriptions, prices, images |
| **Team** | Team member profiles and photos |
| **Testimonials** | Client reviews shown on the homepage |
| **FAQs** | Questions and answers on the FAQ page |
| **Treatment Gallery** | Before/after and treatment photos |

---

## Site Settings

This is where your core clinic information lives. Click **Site Settings** in the sidebar.

Things you can update here:
- **Clinic name and tagline** — shown in the hero on the homepage
- **Opening hours** — displayed on the contact page
- **Social media links** — Instagram, Facebook etc.
- **Contact details** — phone number, email, address

After making changes, click the **Publish** button in the bottom right. Changes won't appear on the website until you publish.

---

## Services (Treatments)

Click **Services** to see all your treatments listed.

Click any treatment to edit it. You can update:
- **Name** — the treatment title
- **Description** — the main body text
- **Price** — shown on the pricing page
- **Duration** — e.g. "60 minutes"
- **Featured** — tick this to show the treatment on the homepage grid
- **Image** — the photo shown on the treatment card and detail page

To add a new treatment, click the **pencil/create** icon at the top of the list.

Always click **Publish** when done.

---

## Team

Click **Team** to manage your team members.

Each team member has:
- **Name** and **job title**
- **Bio** — a short description about them
- **Photo** — their profile image
- **Order** — controls the order they appear on the team page (1 = first)

To add a new team member, click the **create** icon at the top.

---

## Testimonials

Click **Testimonials** to manage client reviews.

Each testimonial has:
- **Client name**
- **Review text**
- **Star rating**
- **Treatment** — which treatment the review relates to (optional)

These appear in the rotating testimonials section on the homepage.

---

## FAQs

Click **FAQs** to manage the questions on your FAQ page.

Each FAQ has:
- **Question**
- **Answer**
- **Order** — controls which order they appear (1 = first)

---

## Treatment Gallery

Click **Treatment Gallery** to add photos to your gallery page.

Each gallery post has:
- **Title** — a short label for the image
- **Category** — e.g. Laser, Skin, Aesthetics (used for filtering)
- **Image** — the photo itself
- **Published date** — controls ordering (most recent first)

---

## Publishing Changes

**Important:** every time you make a change, you must click **Publish** for it to appear on the live website.

- The **Publish** button is in the bottom right corner of each document
- If you see a blue **Publish** button, there are unpublished changes
- If it says **Published** in grey, the content is already live

Changes typically appear on the website within a few minutes of publishing.
